About Us.

Atelier Twenty Four is a design practice working at the intersection of architecture and interior design, creating spaces that prioritize clarity, craft, and a quiet sense of refinement.

We approach each project as a conversation between materiality and light, believing that architecture should feel effortless rather than imposed. Our work focuses on restraint — not as limitation, but as a means to bring forward what matters: proportion, texture, detail.

Based in New Delhi, we work with residential and commercial clients who value thoughtful design, timeless aesthetics, and spaces that feel both intentional and inviting.
