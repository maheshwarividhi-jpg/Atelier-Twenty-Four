Ayush Kalra

Principal Architect & Designer

Studied at the Faculty of Architecture, KU Leuven, and worked across Europe before returning to New Delhi to establish Atelier Twenty Four in 2017.

His time abroad shaped more than his aesthetic — it fundamentally changed how he approaches the act of designing. Working alongside peers who began with ambition rather than caution taught him that the best outcomes are rarely the most obvious ones. He brought that ethos back, and built a practice around it.

With a decade and a half in the industry, his work is defined by an obsession with materials, craft, and the kind of detail that is noticed only when it is missing. Every project is held to a process as considered as the design itself — because how something gets built is inseparable from what it becomes.
