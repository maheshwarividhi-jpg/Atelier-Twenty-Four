Curated Spaces.

Defined by Purpose.

Atelier Twenty Four is an architecture and interior design studio built on the belief that the most considered spaces are also the quietest ones — where nothing calls attention to itself, yet everything is felt.

We work at an intimate scale, across homes, workplaces, hospitality and retail — from the first sketch to the final finish. This end-to-end process is deliberate. It is how intention survives execution.

Every space we take on carries its own set of demands — what it must hold, what it must let go. Our work is the careful negotiation between the two.

We take on few projects at a time. Deliberately so.

Every room holds a version of the life meant to be lived inside it. Our work is simply the act of finding it.
