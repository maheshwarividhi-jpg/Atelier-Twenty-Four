About

Located in New Delhi, this office was designed for a growing beauty and cosmetics brand, with the spatial planning centered around the NPD (New Product Development) room. Conceived as the core of the workplace, the NPD space is enclosed with translucent walls, allowing it to remain visually connected to the rest of the office while maintaining a sense of focus and privacy.

The overall design language follows a minimal and contemporary approach. Clean lines, muted tones, and soft textures create a calm and efficient working environment, allowing the architecture and spatial planning to define the character of the workplace.

Curved transitions, integrated lighting, and understated materials introduce subtle movement within the otherwise linear layout. The result is a modern office environment that feels open, organized, and quietly refined—supporting both collaboration and focused product development.
