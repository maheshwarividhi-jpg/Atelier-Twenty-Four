About

Situated in New Delhi, this apartment was designed as a contemporary home defined by clarity of form and a restrained material palette. The client envisioned a space that feels minimal yet expressive, where architectural elements subtly shape the character of the interiors.

Curved surfaces, perforated walls, and built-in seating niches introduce gentle sculptural moments within the otherwise clean spatial framework. Soft neutral tones and diffused lighting enhance the sense of calm, while carefully integrated details add depth without overwhelming the simplicity of the design.

The result is a modern living environment that balances minimalism with warmth—creating interiors that feel refined, comfortable, and quietly distinctive.
