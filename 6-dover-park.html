6 Dover
Park.

Category

Residential

Location

Kolkata

Scope

Interior Design & Architecture

Year

2021

A layered dialogue between heritage and modernity, 6 Dover Park weaves teak joinery, hand-curated antiques, and textured lime plaster into a home that breathes with memory. Across three floors and an open terrace, each space unfolds with quiet intention — from the dramatic wrought-iron entry gate to the double-height stairwell crowned in tropical light, every detail is a considered act of living.
